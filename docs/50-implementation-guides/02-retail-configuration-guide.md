# Retail Configuration Guide

## 1. Overview

This guide explains how to configure a new location for a retail store.

## 2. Steps

### Step 1: Create Product Categories

1.  Navigate to the "Products" > "Categories" section of the admin backoffice.
2.  Click on the "New Category" button.
3.  Enter the name of the category (e.g., "T-Shirts", "Pants") and click "Create".

### Step 2: Create Products

1.  Navigate to the "Products" > "Products" section.
2.  Click on the "New Product" button.
3.  Enter the name, description, and category of the product.
4.  If the product has variants (e.g., different sizes or colors), add them in the "Variants" section.
5.  Click "Create".

### Step 3: Configure Registers

1.  Navigate to the "Locations" > "Registers" section.
2.  Click on the "New Register" button.
3.  Enter the name of the register (e.g., "Front Counter") and click "Create".
