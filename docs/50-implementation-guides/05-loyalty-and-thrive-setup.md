# Loyalty and Thrive Setup

## 1. Overview

This guide explains how to set up the loyalty and Zoho Thrive integration.

## 2. Steps

### Step 1: Configure the Zoho Thrive Connector

1.  Navigate to the "Integrations" section of the admin backoffice.
2.  Select "Zoho Thrive".
3.  Enter your Zoho Thrive API key and organization ID.

### Step 2: Configure Loyalty Program Rules

1.  In your Zoho Thrive account, configure the rules for your loyalty program. This includes how many points customers earn for their purchases, what rewards they can redeem, and what tiers they can achieve.

### Step 3: Test the Integration

1.  Enroll a new customer in the loyalty program from the POS.
2.  Make a sale to the customer and verify that they earn points in their Zoho Thrive account.
3.  Redeem a reward for the customer and verify that their points are deducted in their Zoho Thrive account.
